Stephen Church

Stephen Church is a Master Cabinetmaker who designs as well as makes fine furniture following the tradition of English 18th century cabinetmakers from Adam and Chippendale to Hepplewhite and Sheraton. Today those traditions are maintained with pride by a selected team of craftsmen working at Theodore Alexander.
