• Undertake any other assignments/responsibilities given by your superiors.

- BA/Computer Science Major/ HCM City University of technology.
- Good communication in English: Reading, Writing, Speaking.

Sub-con Manager

Subcontractor Manager JD

As Subcontract Managers, or Subcontract Administrators, you are responsible for developing and negotiating subcontracts: capability, production timeline; analyzing outsources demand: type of products, quantity & schedule... Distribute orders to suitable adaptability vendors and follow up until orders get delivered to company. Coordinating with related department to get order processing flow as procedure, systemize, timeline control. Using administrative expertise and contract-management skills, Subcontract Manager evaluates performance of employees and monitor supply chain with their company.

Day-to-Day Duties

• Develop subcontract specifications, terms and conditions, as well as modify, add or delete portions of the subcontract to suit your company or vendors' specifications.
• Confer with staff and vendors, interviews vendors, attends internal meeting about outsourcing demand.
• Distribute orders as of vendor capability; competitive business term..
• Coordinate and supervise purchasing clerks to assure POs are aligned with vendor capacity and timeline, MOQ & other agreement.
• Visit vendor to follow up order progress; address issue; communicate, coordinate internal technically dept. get support to solve problem. Assure the flow until delivery.
• Get involved, coordinated for solving any issue after delivering, such as quality issue…Discuss, negotiate with both side to fix problem at the sense of win-win mindset.
• Evaluate vendor performance; fade out bad performing vendors. Discuss, negotiate to get better priority, gain business, and maintain relationship with loyal, good service vendors…

Management Expertise

Subcontract management career require you:
• Have strong observation and communication skills. You must be able to establish and maintain interpersonal relationships, as well as handle complaints, settle disputes and resolve conflicts.
• Must have strong decision-making skills, as well as the ability to organize and prioritize work.
• Need the ability to analyze data and identify the reasons behind specific decisions within subcontracts.
• Be able to monitor the subcontract process, review materials and organize events.

Education and Training

• Business Administration
• Negotiation skill win- win
• Organizing, structure mind set
• Dynamite, open mind set
• Be firmed, clear on communication, get to the end…
• Scheduling, project management
